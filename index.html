Methodology: Each year's 100 points are distributed proportionally across Melbourne suburbs by their weighted hipster venue score. 2014 anchor: 24 weekly Broadsheet Melbourne scrapes (Jul 2014 – Feb 2015) via Kimonolabs, 10 pts per suburb per top-9 appearance. 2026 anchor: curated weighted counts of hipster-relevant venue types per suburb (cafes, bars, wine bars, vintage, galleries, record stores, bookshops, cycle shops, craft breweries). Intermediate years 2016–2024: logistic-curve interpolation with suburb-specific gentrification trajectories and a COVID-19 dip for 2020.

Original project by mattwl (2015). Rebuilt 2026 with D3.js v7 and Bootstrap 5. Intermediate-year scores are modelled, not measured.
